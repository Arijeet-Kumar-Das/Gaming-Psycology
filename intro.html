A Basic Introduction

Psychology in video games is a fascinating and complex topic that examines the intersection of video games and human psychology. Video games have become a significant part of modern culture, with millions of people playing games daily. As such, the impact of video games on human psychology has become an increasingly important area of study.

Psychology in video games explores how video games can impact players' mental health and well-being, from the positive effects on cognitive skills to the negative effects of video game addiction.
